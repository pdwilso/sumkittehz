The Equal Rights Amendment

Thirty-eight states have ratiied the Equal Rights Amendment (ERA) . On January 27th, 2020 the thirty-eighth state ratified the ERA, yet now, in March of 2023, the current U.S. administration is blocking ERA from being added to the U.S. Constitution , and sitting Senators are blatantly defying the Will of We The People in their attempt to prevent the now fully ratified ERA from becoming Law as part of the US Constitution.

What can we do to prevent the administration and Congress from derailing the ERA yet again in the 21st Century?

Join Us! Equal Means Equal is fighting to get the ERA accepted as Law. Join us in the fight to secure for all persons the Rights enumberated by the ERA.

Equal Means Equal: The Documentary

The documentary film production EQUAL MEANS EQUAL provides an unflinching look at how women are treated in the United States today. By following both real life stories and precedent setting legal cases, director Kamala Lopez discovers how outdated and discriminatory attitudes inform and influence seemingly disparate issues, from workplace matters to domestic violence, rape and sexual assault to the foster care system, the healthcare system and the legal system. Lopez reveals the inadequacy of laws currently in place in the US - laws that claim to protect women, but which too often fall far short of that goal.
